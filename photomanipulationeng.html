COMPETITION TERMS AND CONDITIONS

COMPETITORS SHOULD READ AND FULLY UNDERSTAND THE RULES AND REGULATIONS THOROUGHLY.

  • Competitors should present their design through an online platform before the deadline.
  • Each competitor is allowed to submit only one design.
  • The initial pictures related to the design should be presented.
  • Responsibility regarding intellectual property rights of the initial pictures is with the designer.
  • The maximum number of allowed initial pictures are 8.
  • The design can be presented in the forms of JPEG or PNG.
  • Designer has the freedom of creating design that provides useful information to the public.
  • Designs that include political agendas or designs related to racism are NOT tolerated and will be rejected from the competition.
  • The idea of the design should be given using a paragraph with less than 60 words and should be submitted as a PDF file.
  • The initial pictures, final design and the PDF containing the idea, should be separately placed inside files and the above-mentioned files should be uploaded as a single archive zip (.rar) file.
  • In the designs, there should not be any kind of logos, copyright marks, identifying marks, borders or any other marks.
  • If needed there should be the possibility of uploading it to the PSD file.
  • The competition is mainly in the forms of “The most attractive” and “The most popular”, and all the designs are considered under these two categories.
  • The most creative design will be selected by the judges and the most popular design will be selected using the amount of Facebook likes.
  • Decision of the judges will be the last decision.
  • Before uploading the design, contestant should register using the official website and obtain registration number information may be provided through the e-mail.
  • The archive (.zip) file should be renamed with the registration number.
  • You must register and obtain a registration number through our website. (https://ruusara.github.io/ruusara/) before uploading. There you will be notified via e-mail.
