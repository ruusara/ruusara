INTRODUCTION

“රූසර-2022, The Digital Art Competition” is organized for the second consecutive time as one of the events which is organized along with “Let My Country Awake”, The Students’ Festival of Literature organized by the Literary Subcommittee of University of Peradeniya. This competition consists of three main categories which are Dubbing, Photo Manipulation, and Comic Character Creation.

TIMELINE

From July 2022 to August 2022

OBJECTIVES

  • To improve the technical and aesthetic knowledge and interest in digital arts.
  • To improve creativity.
  • To exhibit the skills in digital arts.
  • To the enjoyment and appreciation of different kinds of digital arts.
