COMPETITION TERMS AND CONDITIONS

EVERYONE PARTICIPATING IN THE COMPETITION SHOULD READ AND UNDERSTAND ALL THE RULES OF THE COMPETITION.

  • Competitors must submit their design online on or before the due date.
  • Only one design can be submitted per team.
  • Candidates can apply individually or in groups, with a maximum of five members per group.
  • Team members can play a video of their choice at will.
  • Political mudslinging should eliminate racist discourse. if such dialogue videos are included, they will be removed from the competition.
  • Do not use obscene words or videos. if such dialogue videos are included, they will be removed from the competition.
  • The duration of the video should be limited to three minutes. Videos longer than three minutes will be removed from the competition.
  • Uploaded video size should be less than 500MB.
  • Videos should be presented in MP4 format. You can use any frame size except 1920*1080.
  • All submitted videos should not have any kind of logo, copyright marks, identifying marks, borders or any other type of markers.
  • The competition consists of two categories: the most creative and the most popular design.
  • The most creative videos will be selected by the jury and the 10 best creative videos will be posted on our Facebook page and the most popular creation will be selected by Facebook likes.
  • The decision of the tribunal is final.
  • THE JURY IS PRIMARILY INVOLVED IN AWARDING POINTS
      The creativity of the video.
      Voice adjustment and control according to the character.
      Flow in video and dialogues.
      The meaning and harmony of the dialogues in the video will be considered.
  • Final design should be uploaded as single zip (.rar) file.
  • Before uploading you need to register through our website (https://ruusara.github.io/ruusara/) and get a registration number. There you will be notified via e-mail.
  • The zip file should be re-registered with the registration number.
  • Only this file should be uploaded through the Google forum provided on the website.
